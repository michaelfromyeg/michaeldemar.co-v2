Leadership

EXERPERIENCED LEADER GUIDED UNDER QUALITY MENTORSHIP.
  • SUNIA Marketing Director (January 2018 — Present)

    Current Marketing director for the Seminar on United Nations and International Affairs (SUNIA), a week-long model United Nations summer camp that takes place in Nordegg, Alberta every August. SUNIA enables over 150 youth from across North America to explore the world of international affairs and learn how to make an impact on the the world around them. Learn more about SUNIA here.

  • Canadian Student Leadership Conference (CSLC) 2018 Executive Director (December 2016 — October 2018)

    For CSLC 2018, I was the director of student programming. My primary responsibility was to oversee the development of program components across the five day conference. I also worked to solidify the conference values, trying the program components together and leaving delegates with a unified message. I had to maintain both vertical and horizontal communication; that is, both within the five collaborating high schools and between them. The task was easily my most difficult and rewarding leadership role, and I am grateful to have been able to impact the delegates who came. Learn more about CSLC here.

  • Edmonton Public School Board Student Senator (September 2017 — June 2019)

    I have served on the EPSB Student Senate for two-terms, engaging students across the district on issues that matter to them. Through their feedback, we have and continue to pursue substantive project work to improve education. In my first year, I was responsible for the graphic design of brochures helping with time management. I am now the vice-chair of the Senate, responsible for aiding meetings and directly supporting the chair. I work directly with students to influence policy that affects their day-to-day student experience, and am privileged to be able act as their voice to the Board of Trustees. I also work on media production related to our district; one example video is viewable here. View the brochures here: Finding Time, Using Time, Your Time. Learn more about the Student Senate here.

  • City of Edmonton Youth Councillor (September 2018 — Present)

    Recently, I have been selected to serve as a committee member on the City of Edmonton Youth Council. I have already been able to engage with youth in Edmonton and better understand what direction they would like to see our city council take. I have assisted in the drafting of memos, reports, and presentations to our city council. Moreover, I have collaborated on many projects, including the CEYC Awards, a first-of-its-kind event to celebrate the youth of our city and recognize their accomplishments. Learn more about the City of Edmonton Youth Council here.

  • Initiative Work (varies)

    Initiative work includes time with Youth Engagement Focus Group (YEFG) team, a student voice initiative that works with McNally's administration; McNally Book Club; MASSIVE Volunteering Club; and #NOGOODWAY campaign. Read more about Massive Volunteering here.
